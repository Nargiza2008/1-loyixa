HEALTHY AND TASTY


Poké (pronounced “POH-keh”)
bowls are healthy, fresh, colorful
and delicious! Made from wholesome, ethically
farmed ingredients, they include everything from avocado and edamame to mushrooms and
mango. Diced, sliced, tossed and served with delicate sauces, they are a bowl of beauty and freshness in
a hectic world. Create your own
combination based on the foods you love, or try one of our in-house classics.
